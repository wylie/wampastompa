Wampa Stompa

Wampa Stompa is inspired by the 2015 YouTube video Darth Punk - The Funk Awakens, which creatively combines Star Wars and Daft Punk. I captured a screenshot from the video to create my own Wampa Stompa mixes, leading to the creation of this site. Initially simple, the site has evolved over the years, and now you can download your own Wampa Stompa Mix! Enjoy!
